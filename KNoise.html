Level guide

These are relative microphone levels on a 0–100 scale, not calibrated decibels. Readings depend on your microphone and where the device sits, so set the thresholds by ear for your own room.

0–25
Near silent
25–45
Quiet individual work
45–70
Group discussion
70+
Too loud to teach over
