Klass

Classroom Tools

Nine simple tools for fair, engaging classroom sessions. Everything runs in your browser and works offline once installed.

Timer

Time a single activity — countdown to a deadline, a quick timer, or presets.

Pomodoro

Run a repeating work-and-break study cycle, with session tracking and daily stats.

Random Name Picker

Pick a student in seconds — the quick, no-fuss picker, with undo and CSV export.

Spinning Wheel

Pick a name the fun way — a big wheel the whole class watches spin.

Team Assignment

Split students into balanced random teams from a pasted list or CSV file.

Presenter Roulette

Spin for a team, then a member — fair rotation for presentations and activities.

Seating Chart

Generate randomized seating charts that maximize distance between students.

QR Sharer

Show a giant QR code on the projector so students can scan links instantly.

Noise Meter

Show classroom noise on screen, with a warning band and an alert when it gets too loud.
