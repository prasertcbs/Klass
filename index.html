Klass

Classroom Tools

Four simple tools for fair, engaging classroom sessions. Everything runs in your browser and works offline once installed.

Random Name Picker

Pick a student at random for classroom participation, with undo and CSV export.

Team Assignment

Split students into balanced random teams from a pasted list or CSV file.

Presenter Roulette

Spin for a team, then a member — fair rotation for presentations and activities.

Seating Chart

Generate randomized seating charts that maximize distance between students.
